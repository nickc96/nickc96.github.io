December 14-15, 2019: Leafeon Sewing Plush

I used the pattern from this website: Eeveelution Sewing Pattern

I worked on this plush as a first time project from a pattern I found online. I ended up giving this as a gift to one of my coworkers whose favorite eeveelution is Leafeon. For a first time attempt, I thought this turned out really well. I used anti-pill plush fabric since it felt soft and it was the one easiest to find for the colors I was looking for. The material is a little thicker, but it results in a good plush. Buying the fabric was actually a weird experience since I had to get a big roll and ask for part to be cut from it to the size I needed. Some small fixes I would make in the future is making my stitches closer and tighter since I had to patch some parts after that showed a little more. I learned that when flipping upside down, back stitching works well. I did have trouble sewing the tail up with a ladder stitch.

One thing I am really proud of is that this inspired my coworker to make a plush herself.
